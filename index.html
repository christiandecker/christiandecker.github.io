Hey there!

I’m a 6th-year PhD Candidate in Economics at the University of Zurich.

My main field of interest is Empirical Industrial Organization; in particular, I’m working on the economics of strategic information disclosure. In my job market paper, I estimate a structural model of Bayesian Persuasion seeking to understand the design of the Airbnb customer rating system. My results reveal how the platform benefits from almost every apartment having a 5-star rating and its consequences relative to a full-information alternative.

My principal supervisor is Greg Crawford.

I am on the academic job market 2022/2023 and available for interviews!
